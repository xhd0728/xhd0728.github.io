# StaffDeck: Enterprise Digital Employee Platform

[ModelBest](https://modelbest.cn/), [NEU-ModelBest Data Intelligence Joint Lab](https://neuir.github.io/), [THUNLP](https://nlp.csai.tsinghua.edu.cn/), [OpenBMB](https://www.openbmb.cn/home), and [AI9Stars](https://github.com/AI9Stars)

<div align="left">
<a href="https://staffdeck.openbmb.cn/"><img src="https://img.shields.io/static/v1?label=Homepage&message=StaffDeck&color=4A6E8B&logo=github-pages" alt="StaffDeck homepage"></a>
<a href="https://staffdeck.openbmb.cn/#/docs/introduce?lang=en"><img src="https://img.shields.io/static/v1?label=Docs&message=StaffDeck&color=4A6E8B&logo=bookstack&logoColor=white" alt="StaffDeck documentation"></a>

StaffDeck is an enterprise platform for building and managing digital employees. It turns professional experience, business processes, and decision criteria into digital employees that can work continuously, take over repetitive tasks, and preserve individual expertise as reusable, evolvable, and traceable organizational assets.

<a href="https://pilotdeck.openbmb.cn/"><img src="https://img.shields.io/static/v1?label=Homepage&message=PilotDeck&color=4A6E8B&logo=github-pages" alt="PilotDeck homepage"></a>
